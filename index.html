The best of both worlds.

Scalar.ly is a fully managed database offering the performance & affordability of NoSQL with the structure & maintainability of SQL. By understanding the read access patterns in advance, our technology is able to optimize the underlying schema, automating the work of a database expert.

We know tech.

With over 15 years of experience building web and mobile products, we are yet to find a problem that we couldn't solve. We have seen it all, from startup to high scale.

  • Optimize through configuration

    Simply declare each type of query you will perform while configuring your database, and Scalar.ly will organize your data storage to maximize performance and minimize cost.

  • Easily scale your data schema

    No more spending valuable engineering resources writing migration scripts. No more thinking about the best way to store your data. Scalar.ly will automatically handle database migrations for you when your config is updated and automatically optimize the storage patterns as needed.

  • Automatically generate libraries

    After your schema is configured, Scalar.ly will generate models in your static language of choice. Manually writing classes to match your data access layer is a thing of the past. Supported formats: TypeScript, Java & Kotlin.
